# SalaryTaxCalc

[![License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC%20BY--NC--SA%204.0-blue.svg)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/)  ![Made by Wang Nan](https://img.shields.io/badge/Made%20by-Wang%20Nan-brightgreen)

本工具用于快速计算薪资劳务的扣税情况，包括：税后工资/劳务、税前工资/劳务（反推）。依据个税政策，包含专项扣除（五险一金）与专项附加扣除。[点击进入](https://tax.wangnan.net)

This tool is designed to quickly calculate the tax deductions on salaries and labor remuneration, including after-tax and pre-tax income (reverse calculation). In accordance with individual income tax policies, it covers special deductions (social insurances and housing fund) and additional special deductions. [Enter](https://tax.wangnan.net)
